Strides Lane Hall Booking

Strides Lane Hall is available for bookings mainly at weekends. See bottom of the page for the calendar with the available times

Address

1st Poulner Scout Group
Strides Lane
Ringwood
BH24 1ED

Facilities include

  • Large hall to accommodate up to 50 people
  • Kitchen
  • Male and Female Toilets
  • Tables, Benches and Chairs
  • Outside Garden Space

Rates

  • £10 per hour before 6:00pm
  • £15 per hour after 6:00pm

A £50 deposit (usually by cheque) is also payable prior to use of the hall to offset any damage that may occur. This cheque is ripped up if there is no damage after the event.
